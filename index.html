Published by ForgettablePublishing and "written" by Anybody, Everybody, and Nobody, Human Guidelines is the ultimate guide to… well, nothing. Because the truth is, there are no universal rules to being human.

This book (or lack of one) is a playful jab at the endless self-help and corporate leadership books promising the right way to be human, when in reality, your humanity is not something to be fixed. It is something to be felt.

Truthfully, if there were a book, it wouldn’t be about fixing you. It would be about feeling, healing, and learning to trust your own wisdom. Being human is not a process to perfect, it is an experience to live.

Whether you are leading a team, leading yourself, or figuring it all out as you go, your journey alone is a book in itself. And while there is no universal guide, there are thousands of beautiful books created by those who forged their own path.
