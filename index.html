Human Guidelines – A Book That Doesn’t Exist (Because It Can’t)

Published by ForgettablePublishing and "written" by Anybody, Everybody, and Nobody, Human Guidelines is the ultimate guide to… well, nothing. Because the truth is, there are no universal rules to being human.

Clicking through to the “real” guide reveals the only wisdom worth knowing: your human experience is yours alone. No one-size-fits-all handbook, no step-by-step manual—just the radical truth that going inward is the only way to create an authentic path.

This book (or lack thereof) is a playful jab at the endless self-help and corporate leadership books promising the "right way" to be human, when in reality, your humanity isn't something to be fixed—it’s something to be felt.

So go ahead, open this book, flip through the invisible pages, and embrace the fact that you’re already the author of your own experience.
